About the Project

Rain It In is a water droplet physics simulation that models the complex behavior of raindrops as they fall, splash upon impact, and interact with various surfaces. Our project implements realistic particle dynamics, splash generation, and fluid behaviors in both 2D and 3D environments.

We utilize physically-based modeling to accurately simulate water droplet interactions, including:

  • Particle dynamics under gravity with fixed-timestep integration
  • Complex splash patterns using Gaussian and log-normal distributions
  • Surface sliding with friction and momentum conservation
  • Puddle formation and water accumulation
  • 3D visualization using OpenGL with advanced lighting and shaders
